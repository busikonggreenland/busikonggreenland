About

Busikong Greenland MPC is facilitated by farming women & men dreaming of leaving a green legacy to the next generation promoting sustainable agriculture The Busikong Greenland MPC was established to support local farmers in 2000 during the height of the All-Out War campaign of the government against the revolutionary groups in Mindanao.

Originally, there were 25 farmers involved in the foundation of the cooperative. The war did not stop the farmers to engage productively on agriculture. The Coop even inspired other farmers within the community and nearby areas to become more focused on farming rather than participating in the conflicts
